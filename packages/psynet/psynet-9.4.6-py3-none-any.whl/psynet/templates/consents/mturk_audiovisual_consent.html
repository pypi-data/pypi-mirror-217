We need your consent to proceed

In this experiment, you may be asked to make a voice or video recording. For example, you may be asked to record your finger-tapping in response to a sound or video, or to record yourself speaking or singing.

Confidentiality

All the recordings we obtain during this research will be kept confidential, and nobody outside the group of researchers will be able to share or store them. However, in some experiments, your recordings may be seen or heard by other participants in order to evaluate them in different attributes or create new materials (for example replicate a rhythm that you created, or continue a melody that you sang). Your recordings will not be associated with your name or other identifiers in any way. Your recordings will not be used to derive your real identity, and they will not be made public. Raw audio/video will not be shared with collaborators (we will only share extracted data from recordings without personal identifiers).

Although researchers will not use your recordings to derive your real identity, personal information could be leaked if you are not in an isolated environment. You may wish to make sure that no sounds/visual material in your environment could be linked to your personal information. In addition, while you perform a recording, the microphone or camera can capture unintended audio or video, for example the camera can capture another family member, or the microphone can capture something you say to another person. For example, you may want to sit in a room that is isolated from the rest of the house. By consenting to this experiment, you are committing not to use profanity while recording audio or nudity while recording video.

Security

During data collection, audio-visual material will be recorded on a separate server and encrypted using a strong encryption protocol (such as Advanced Encryption Standard). MTurk ID information will not be used to index this data.

Consent

By consenting to participate, you acknowledge that you are 18 years or older, have read this consent form, agree to its contents, and agree to take part in this research. If you do not wish to consent, please close this page and return the HIT on Mechanical Turk.
